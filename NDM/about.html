Features

Our Crisis Support section offers a suite of essential services designed to enhance disaster management. Real-Time Crisis Support (RTCS) provides instant communication tools and live updates, facilitating rapid response and coordination during emergencies. Resource Coordination ensures the efficient allocation and distribution of critical supplies, minimizing waste. Lost and Found Services centralize the reporting and tracking of missing persons and items, aiding in family reunification and recovery. These features, along with future additions, are crafted to deliver comprehensive and effective support in times of crisis.
